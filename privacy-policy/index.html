Privacy Policy – Neuron Battery Monitor

Effective Date: October 14, 2025

1. Data Collection and Use

Neuron – Battery Monitor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or device data. All battery information (such as power, voltage, current, or temperature) is processed locally on your device and never sent to any server or third party. We do not collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.), location or network data, or advertising identifiers.

2. Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no information is shared with any external parties, including advertisers, analytics services, or cloud providers.

3. Permissions and Device Access

The App may request basic system permissions (such as battery status or charging state) only to display accurate information about your device’s performance. These permissions never transmit or store data externally.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all age groups (rated “3+”). We do not collect personal information from any users, including children.

5. Data Security

As no personal data is transmitted or stored, there is no user data exposed to unauthorized access. All operations occur entirely on your local device.

6. Third-Party Services (Huawei AppGallery)

While our App does not collect or process any personal data, Huawei AppGallery or related Huawei services may collect limited information for app distribution, crash reporting, or updates according to their own policies. You can review Huawei’s official Privacy Policy here: https://consumer.huawei.com/en/privacy/privacy-policy/ .

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on our official listing page, with a new effective date. Please check periodically for the latest version.
